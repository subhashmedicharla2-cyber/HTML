Biography about subhash chandra bose

Subhash chandra bose was a very important leaderin India's fight for independence. He was a brave and influential figure who inspired many people.

achievments

Subhas Chandra Bose (Netaji) was a legendary Indian freedom fighter who led the Indian National Army (INA) and founded the Provisional Government of Free India. His radical approach, international diplomacy, and fiery leadership significantly pressured the British to leave India.

birth and death

Subhash Chandra Bose was born on January 23, 1897, in Cuttack, Odisha, and is presumed to have died on August 18, 1945, from third-degree burns following a reported plane crash in Taihoku, Taiwan.
